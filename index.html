Welcome to the Playlistle Beta! This is a song-guessing game to test your knowledge to see how well you know your music!
Copy the link to a playlist (make sure its public and under 300 songs, we're working to increase that number!) and put it into the box below!
You'll have three guesses to guess the song:
Guess 1: 0.5 second clip: 30 points
Guess 2: 1 second clip: 20 points
Guess 3: 3 second clip: 10 points
Each run of the game, you get 3 lives. When you fail to guess a song correctly or skip an entire song, you lose a life
Keep going until you run out of lives, and then share your highest scores!

If you find a bug, please report it to @theastra_ or @nasafrasa on discord!
